User Guide

As a user, you can join tournaments and participate. All commands must be sent in the channel where the tournament was created.

!join [username] or !j [username]
Join the tournament. Username is only necessary (and required) if the tournament is using ratings from chess.com or lichess.org.

!pairing or !p
Get your current match in the tournament.

!info or !status
Provides a link to view the current pairings and standings.

!report w-l[-d] or !results w-l[-d] or !r w-l[-d]
Report results for your match. Wins are how many games you won. Draws are only required if greater than 0.

!quit or !q
Quit the tournament. If the tournament has started, you will be dropped.
